Our standard machine requires a 3×3 ft footprint – about the size of a small high-top table. We’ll help you tuck it into a corner, near an entry, or by the restrooms so it earns without getting in the way.
What kind of money are we talking about? +
Exact numbers depend on your traffic and local demand, but the goal is simple: generate meaningful incremental profit from space that isn’t earning today. Because we handle inventory, processing, and shrink, your portion is truly passive income.
Who’s responsible for compliance and permits? +
NightCap Vapor manages all machine-related compliance and required retailer permits connected to our operations. We design the program to integrate cleanly with your existing obligations as a 21+ venue.
Does this create more work for my staff? +
No. Your team doesn’t process sales, stock product, or troubleshoot the machine. If something ever needs attention, we handle it.
